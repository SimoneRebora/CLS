## Computational Literary Studies Projects

### Distant Reading for European Literary History

A network of researchers jointly developing the resources and methods for the computational study of European literary history.  
***Funded by:*** European Commission  
***Duration**:* 2017-2022  
***Link:*** [https://www.distant-reading.net/](https://www.distant-reading.net/)

### SPP (Schwerpunktprogramme) 2207 Computational Literary Studies

A priority programme dedicated to developig and establishing innovative computational methods in the field of literary studies.  
***Funded by:*** German Research Foundation (DFG)  
***Duration:*** 2019-  
***Link:*** [https://dfg-spp-cls.github.io/](https://dfg-spp-cls.github.io/)

### Computational Literary Studies Infrastructure (CLS INFRA)

A four-year partnership to build a shared resource of high-quality data, tools and knowledge to aid new approaches to studying literature in the digital age.  
***Funded by:*** European Commission  
***Duration:*** 2021-2025  
***Link:*** [https://clsinfra.io/](https://clsinfra.io/)
